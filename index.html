About Nexora

Nexora Impact Technologies is a global innovation company focused on building advanced technological solutions that generate measurable social, economic, and environmental impact. Our mission is to empower organizations, governments, and communities through artificial intelligence, digital transformation, and data-driven innovation. Through strategic partnerships, research, and technology development, Nexora works at the intersection of technology and global development to tackle complex challenges such as climate change, digital inequality, and sustainable economic growth.

Leadership

Frederik Bjørn

Founder & CEO


Frederik Bjørn is the founder and CEO of Nexora Impact Technologies. Born in 1989, Frederik is an entrepreneur and technology strategist focused on building innovative digital ecosystems that empower global communities. His leadership focuses on bridging cutting-edge technologies with real-world impact, bringing together artificial intelligence, global partnerships, and sustainable innovation.
